Mission


Our research proposal is to develop a novel technqiue for modelling the spread of wildfires within urban, Mediterranean communities. Wildfires are catasrophic and numerous. It is the duty of communities living on the edge of hinterlands to contribute postively to fuel management and reductions to prevent the spread of wildfires.


Working with families, fire districts, HOAs, regional park managers, foresters, and fire chiefs, we have developed a free webmap service to collect the necessary spatial information needed in simulating wildfire propogation in this wildland urban interface. This service is designed for large communities to collectively identify, confirm, and report the vegetated and non-vegetated fuels in their neighborhoods. Recording your observations, as a citizen scientist, will contribute to the creation of a personalized wildfire mitigation strategy for you and your community.
